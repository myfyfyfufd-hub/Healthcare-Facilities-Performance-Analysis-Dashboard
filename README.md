# Healthcare Facilities Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes healthcare facilities across Pakistan to understand healthcare infrastructure, workforce capacity, emergency service availability, and facility performance. The dashboard was developed using Power BI and provides interactive visualizations that help identify healthcare trends, resource distribution, and service quality insights.

---

## Business Problem

Healthcare organizations require accurate information about facility capacity, staffing levels, emergency services, and healthcare quality. Understanding these factors is essential for improving patient care, optimizing resource allocation, and supporting strategic healthcare planning.

This project transforms healthcare facility data into actionable business insights through data analysis and visualization.

---

## Business Questions

1. How many healthcare facilities are operating?
2. What is the total healthcare bed capacity available?
3. How many doctors, nurses, and staff support healthcare services?
4. Which provinces have the highest number of healthcare facilities?
5. Which cities have the strongest healthcare infrastructure?
6. What percentage of facilities provide emergency services?
7. What percentage of facilities are highly rated?
8. Which facility types perform best?
9. How does healthcare quality vary across regions?
10. What opportunities exist to improve healthcare service delivery?

## Key Findings

* Healthcare infrastructure is distributed across multiple provinces and cities.
* Workforce capacity plays a critical role in healthcare service delivery.
* Emergency service availability varies among healthcare facilities.
* Facility ratings highlight differences in healthcare quality and operational performance.
* Regional analysis reveals opportunities for healthcare investment and service improvement.

---

## Recommendations

* Increase healthcare investment in underserved regions.
* Expand emergency service coverage where needed.
* Improve workforce distribution across facilities.
* Monitor facility ratings to maintain healthcare quality standards.
* Use healthcare performance metrics for long-term planning and resource allocation.
